KolKol

קול-קול kolkol.co.il AVAILABLE
"Kol" (קול) means "voice" and "sound" in Hebrew. Doubled = "every voice, every sound." Deep Hebrew meaning + playful repetition = perfect dual-language brand. Also echoes "call-call" in English. The most culturally rooted name that still feels modern, playful, and internationally appealing.
