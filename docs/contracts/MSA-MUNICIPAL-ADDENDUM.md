# MSA Addendum — Municipal Conservatorium Clauses

**Addendum version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-03-20
**Status:** Draft — Requires review by Israeli legal counsel before use
**Incorporates:** Lyriosa MSA-TEMPLATE.md as base agreement

> This addendum supplements the Lyriosa Master Service Agreement (MSA-TEMPLATE.md) for contracts with municipal entities (עיריות, מועצות מקומיות, ותאגידים עירוניים). In case of conflict, this addendum prevails.

---

## Addendum A — Municipal-Specific Definitions

**"Municipal Authority"** means the municipality, regional council, or municipal corporation (תאגיד עירוני) that is party to this Agreement on behalf of the Conservatorium.

**"Budget Committee Approval"** means the formal resolution of the Municipal Authority's finance/budget committee authorizing this expenditure.

**"Auditor"** means the State Comptroller (מבקר המדינה), the Municipal Internal Auditor (מבקר הרשות), or any other authorized audit authority.

---

## Addendum B — Budget Committee Approval

### B.1 Condition Precedent
This Agreement shall not become binding until the Municipal Authority has provided Lyriosa with written evidence of Budget Committee approval for the contract value specified in the Order Form.

### B.2 Annual Renewal Authorization
For multi-year contracts, the Municipal Authority shall provide renewed Budget Committee approval before the start of each contract year. Failure to provide such approval by the renewal date entitles Lyriosa to suspend services with 30 days' notice.

### B.3 Budget Reduction
If the municipal budget is reduced during a contract term such that the Conservatorium cannot fulfill payment obligations:
- The Municipal Authority shall notify Lyriosa within 14 days of receiving the revised budget
- The parties shall negotiate a modified scope or pricing in good faith
- If no agreement is reached within 60 days, either party may terminate with 90 days' notice

---

## Addendum C — Auditor Access

### C.1 State Comptroller and Internal Auditor
Lyriosa agrees to provide the following to authorized auditors upon written request:
- Access to Lyriosa's financial records related to fees paid under this Agreement
- Documentation of services provided
- System access logs relevant to the Conservatorium's data
- Copies of DPAs and security certifications

### C.2 Audit Process
- Auditor requests shall be made in writing with at least 14 days' notice
- Lyriosa may redact information that would compromise the security of other customers
- Audit access shall be limited to information directly related to the Municipal Authority's contract

### C.3 Subcontractor Audits
Lyriosa's right to audit its own sub-processors (including Supabase, Vercel) shall be exercised on behalf of the Municipal Authority in accordance with those sub-processors' standard DPA terms.

## Addendum D — Annual Renewal and Pricing

### D.1 Renewal Notice Period
For municipal contracts, the renewal notice period is extended to 120 days (vs. 60 days in the base MSA) to accommodate the municipal budget planning cycle.

### D.2 Price Adjustment Cap
Annual price increases shall not exceed the lesser of:
- The Israeli Consumer Price Index (CPI) change for the preceding calendar year, plus 3%
- 8% of the prior year's contract value

### D.3 Multi-Year Price Lock
At the Municipal Authority's request, Lyriosa will offer fixed pricing for up to 3 years in exchange for a firm multi-year commitment.

## Addendum E — Data Sovereignty and Privacy

### E.1 Data Residency
All personal data of Israeli citizens processed under this Agreement shall be stored within the European Economic Area (EEA) or in a jurisdiction recognized as providing adequate protection under Israeli law. Lyriosa currently stores data in Supabase (EU-West-1, Frankfurt).

### E.2 Municipal Records Law
The Municipal Authority acknowledges that Lyriosa is a data processor (מעבד) as defined under PDPPA, and that the Municipal Authority remains the data controller (בעל מאגר). All data stored in Lyriosa belongs to the Municipal Authority and shall be returned or deleted per Article 6 of the base MSA.

### E.3 FOIA / Freedom of Information
Lyriosa acknowledges that municipal contracts may be subject to freedom of information requests (חוק חופש המידע). The Municipal Authority shall give Lyriosa advance notice of any FOIA request relating to this Agreement to allow Lyriosa to seek protection of commercially sensitive information.

## Addendum F — Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

### F.1 Governing Law
This Addendum and the base MSA, as amended, are governed by the laws of the State of Israel.

### F.2 Disputes — Mandatory Mediation
Before initiating arbitration or litigation, the parties shall attempt mediation before a neutral mediator agreed upon by both parties, for a period of 45 days.

### F.3 Jurisdiction
The competent courts in Tel Aviv, Israel shall have exclusive jurisdiction over disputes not resolved through mediation.

---

## Addendum G — Insurance Requirements

The Municipal Authority may require Lyriosa to maintain:
- Professional liability (E&O) insurance: minimum ₪5,000,000 per claim
- Cyber liability insurance: minimum ₪3,000,000 per incident
- General third-party liability: minimum ₪5,000,000 per claim

Lyriosa shall provide certificates of insurance upon request.
