## Scope of Authority

The DSO is responsible for:

1. **Policy oversight** — Maintaining and updating the Information Security Policy
2. **Risk assessment** — Conducting annual Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs) for all modules processing personal data
3. **Incident response** — Leading the response to data security incidents and breaches
4. **Regulatory compliance** — Monitoring compliance with PDPPA, Amendment 40 (spam law), and related regulations
5. **Sub-processor oversight** — Reviewing and approving DPAs with all sub-processors
6. **Training** — Ensuring all staff handling personal data receive appropriate training
7. **DSAR handling** — Overseeing responses to Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) within 30 days
