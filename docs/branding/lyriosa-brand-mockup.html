Why Lyriosa works for this platform

Musical roots — "Lyri-" from lyre (oldest string instrument) and lyrical (poetic, expressive). The "-osa" suffix evokes Italian musical terms: arioso, grazioso, maestoso.

International reach — Works natively in all four platform locales. No awkward transliterations. Hebrew ליריוסה reads naturally and sounds warm.
Visual identity continuity — Inherits the proven Harmonia indigo design system (#4338CA primary, Playfair + Jakarta Sans, glass stats bar). Zero design debt from the rename.

Institutional credibility — Sophisticated enough for Ministry interfaces, warm enough for parent-facing enrollment. Balances government trust with creative spirit.
