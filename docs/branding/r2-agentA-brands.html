Etymology

From Hebrew mizmor (מזמור) -- a psalm or musical composition, and mezammer (מזמר) -- "one who sings/plays." Deep roots in Jewish musical tradition. The word appears 57 times in the Book of Psalms.

Why It Works

6 letters. Feels professional and established. The "zmar" sound is distinctly Semitic yet pronounceable globally. Bridges ancient tradition with modern tech. Appeals to parents and administrators who want gravitas.

IP Check

mizmar.io is taken (Arabic music blog). almizmar.com exists (Arabic reed instrument). But mizmar.co.il + .com are clear. Different enough from existing Arabic usage. Strong for Israeli market.
